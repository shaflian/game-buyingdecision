IVHype vs. Reality20%

The gap between press scores and player reviews. A large split means marketing overpromised — that gap gets penalised.

avg(critic, player) − gap × 0.4

Critics 86 · Players 71Δ15 — slight gap
Critics 90 · Players 55Δ35 — major split ✗
